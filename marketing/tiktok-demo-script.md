# 🎬 TikTok App Review - Demo Video Script

## Video Requirements (from TikTok)
- Show complete end-to-end flow
- Demonstrate Content Posting API integration
- Show user interface and interactions clearly
- Max 50MB per video

## Recording Setup
- **Tool**: QuickTime Player (File > New Screen Recording) or Loom
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080 recommended
- **Duration**: 60-90 seconds
- **Format**: MP4

---

## 📋 Demo Script (Step by Step)

### Scene 1: Landing Page (5 seconds)
**URL**: `https://n01.app`

**Action**: Show the homepage briefly
**Narration** (optional): "n01.app is an AI-powered content creation platform"

---

### Scene 2: Navigate to TikTok Integration (5 seconds)
**URL**: `https://n01.app/integrations/tiktok`

**Action**: 
1. Scroll down slightly to show the page
2. Highlight the "TikTok Integration" text

**What to show**:
- Page title "Publish AI-Generated Videos Directly to TikTok"
- The TikTok Account connection section

---

### Scene 3: Connect TikTok Account (10 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Click the "Connect TikTok" button
2. Wait for the connection animation
3. Show the "Connected as @your_account" status

**What to show**:
- The OAuth flow simulation
- Green "✓ Connected" badge appearing

---

### Scene 4: Show Video Queue (10 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Scroll down to "Ready to Publish" section
2. Hover over the video cards

**What to show**:
- AI-generated video thumbnails
- "Generated by PIXEL" label
- "Ready" status badge
- "Publish to TikTok" button

---

### Scene 5: Open Publish Modal (15 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Click "Publish to TikTok" on the first video
2. Show the modal opening

**What to show**:
- Video preview
- Caption input field
- Privacy setting dropdown (Public/Friends/Private)
- "Allow comments" checkbox
- "Allow duets" checkbox

---

### Scene 6: Fill Publishing Options (15 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Type a caption: "Check out our latest product! #ai #tech"
2. Select privacy setting (Public)
3. Ensure checkboxes are checked

**What to show**:
- User typing in the caption field
- Dropdown selection
- Checkbox interactions

---

### Scene 7: Publish Video (15 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Click "Publish Now" button
2. Show the loading state ("Publishing...")
3. Wait for success message

**What to show**:
- Loading spinner animation
- Success screen with green checkmark
- "Published Successfully!" message
- "Your video is now live on TikTok" text

---

### Scene 8: Show Legal Links (10 seconds)
**Action**:
1. Scroll down to footer
2. Highlight Terms of Service and Privacy Policy links

**What to show**:
- Footer with visible links
- Terms: https://n01.app/terms
- Privacy: https://n01.app/privacy

---

### Scene 9: End Screen (5 seconds)
**Action**: 
1. Scroll back to top
2. Show the connected status

**Final frame**: n01.app logo with connected TikTok status

---

## 🎯 Key Points to Highlight

1. **User Authentication**: Show the "Connect TikTok" button and successful connection
2. **Content Creation**: Show AI-generated videos ready for publishing
3. **User Control**: Demonstrate that users choose what to publish (caption, privacy, etc.)
4. **Publishing Flow**: Show the complete publish action with confirmation
5. **Legal Compliance**: Show Terms and Privacy links in footer
